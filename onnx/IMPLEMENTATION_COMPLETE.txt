================================================================================
ONNX STRUCTURE
================================================================================

ALGO STAGE:
Input: RGB/YUV image [n,3,h,w]
Process: ReduceMean → Constant → Div → Log → Clip → Exp
Output: stats, target, brightness_ratio, exposure_value, gain

APPLIER STAGE:
Input: Image [n,3,h,w] + gain coefficient
Process: Mul → Clip
Output: Exposure-compensated image [n,3,h,w]
